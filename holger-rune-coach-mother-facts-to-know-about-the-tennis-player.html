Holger Rune Coach Mother: Facts To Know About The Tennis Player

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Holger Rune has cited his coach mother, Aneke, as his biggest inspiration in his tennis career. Learn more about the mother-son duo’s rapport below!

Holger Rune is a Danish professional tennis player. He is considered to be one of the well-known youngest players in the field and is expected to have a very bright future ahead.

Having turned professional at the age of 16 in 2020, he has acquired quite a name for himself.

He is currently competing at the Grand Slam tennis tournament, where he lost his maiden match as of today.

Meet Holger Rune Coach Mother

Holger Rune attained his coach mother, Aneke, after he sought interest in tennis at the young age of six.

His mother trained him throughout his childhood, along with another tennis coach, Lars Christensen.

It is definitely his mother’s sheer work and his passion for the game that he has attained such achievements as of today. Moreover, his family has a history of tennis.

His sister Alma is also a tennis player who was the trigger for his interest in the sport.

With such supporting family background, it was for sure that  Holger would attain such a feat at a young age.

What Is Holger Rune Height?

Holger Rune stands at a towering height of more than 6 feet 1 inch at such a budding age. His height and body build have surely helped him in his athletic career.

Holger Rune Age Revealed

Holger Rune’s current age is just 18 years old as of 2021. He was born in 2003 in Gentofte.

His birthday falls on April 29 every year with his family and relatives. He became the youngest Danish male singles champion by winning the 2019 indoor tournament at the age of 15.

Holger also achieved an ITF junior ranking of No. 1 in the world after winning at the ITF Junior Finals on October 28, 2019, when he was 16 years old.

Follow Holger Rune On Instagram

The tennis prodigy Rune can be traced down on Instagram under the username @holgerrune with a verified tick on the side of his profile.

More than 81k followers follow him as of currently. He mostly updates on his career and personal life too.

According to his Instagram posts, he seems very close with his sister Alma. They are often seen together in the photos where they share vacations, festivals, and family gatherings.
