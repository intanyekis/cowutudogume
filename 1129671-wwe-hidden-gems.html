Adrian Street Overseas Championship Defense Headed To WWE Network Hidden Gems

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 1, 2024

More Adrian Street content is headed to the WWE Network, this time in the Hidden Gems section of the platform. According to a new report from WWE Network News, three videos from Street defending his title will come to the Network, with one from Stampede and the other two coming from Mid-South Wrestling.

Regarding the report, it seems as if both Mid-South contests will show the entirety of the match – with Street going up against Bobby Fulton and Terry Taylor, respectively – while the Stampede match is just a brief, three-minute long clip. The shortened clip shows Street going up against Davey Boy Smith, and is likely just a television highlight cobbled together from other footage.

For a breakdown of all six gems coming to the Hidden Gems section on Thursday, check out below courtesy of WWE Network News:


Two of Britain’s best square off when Adrian Street faces Davey Boy Smith from Stampede Wrestling.


“Exotic” Adrian Street battles Bobby Fulton of The Fantastics in this exciting match from Mid-South Wrestling.


Terry Taylor takes on one of wrestling’s most interesting characters in Adrian Street in this bout from Mid-South Wrestling.


The Undertaker defends his WWE Championship against the British Bulldog in this rarely seen contest from Canada.


Randy Orton looks to successfully defend his WWE Championship against Triple H across the pond.


Finn Bálor puts the NXT Championship on the line against Samoa Joe and Sami Zayn in a Triple Threat Match.
