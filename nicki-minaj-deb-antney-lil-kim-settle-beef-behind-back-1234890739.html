Nicki Minaj Blasts Deb Antney For Trying To Settle Lil Kim Beef

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Nicki Minaj is upset with her former manager, Deb Antney, after the music executive secretly reached out to Lil Kim in hopes to settle their longtime beef. However, Minaj made it clear that not only did she never ask her to do that, but also she’d “never want that” anyway.

Antney, who’s also Waka Flocka’s mom, revealed during a recent interview with Opinionated Truths Podcast that she extended an olive branch to the rap vet, unbeknownst to Minaj.

“I think that we all would love to see that but people would have to be willing to do that,” she explained when asked if she tried to squash their beef. “And to be perfectly honest with you, and [Nicki Minaj] gonna kill me for saying even this, but I did reach out to Kim.” 

Although Antney’s intentions seemed to be in the right place, the Pink Friday 2 icon wasn’t having it. She took to X on Sunday (June 30), sounding off about how people would think Antney is speaking on her behalf and also promoting herself in between.

“Deb, Isn’t it funny how you left out the biggest part of the story?” she began while also mentioning that the second installment of her North American tour would begin in September. “I didn’t ask you to do a THING! You told me AFTER THE FACT & I SCREAMED ON YOU & told you if you reach out to ppl now that you’re being seen around me, they will THINK it’s coming FROM ME so to NEVER do that again w/o discussing it with me FIRST.”

“I said I’d never want that & that now she’d think that message came from me which is not cool,” she added.

Also mentioning that Antney had asked her to bring out Foxy and Kim on her historic “Top 10 HIGHEST GROSSING TOUR BY A FEMALE RAPPER,” she added, “You laughed it off & said her & foxy should come out I said no I’d only want foxy to come out on stage with me, she’s like family to me. No disrespect.”

“God bless everyone & our children!!! Let us all do our thing & thank God for LIFE. Stop using my name for clickbait in 2024. We get it!!!!! I’m dat btch!!!!!!” she declared. “How dare you? Interviews? Chiiiiiiiiiiiiii WOO WEE!!!!! This world, man, this world. Love yourself deeply.” 

She also made sure to promote her LØCI sneakers, Pink Friday Nails, and signature Pink Friday fragrance.
