Rahul Bose Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Rahul Bose, born on 27 July 1967, is a multi-faceted personality known for his roles as an Indian film actor, director, screenwriter, social activist, and rugby player. Bose has made significant contributions to Bengali and Hindi cinema, with notable appearances in films such as Mr. and Mrs. Iyer, Pyaar Ke Side Effects, The Japanese Wife, and Vishwaroopam. Recognized for his work in parallel cinema, he has been lauded by publications like Time and Maxim which described him as “the superstar of Indian arthouse cinema” and “the Sean Penn of Oriental cinema” respectively. Apart from his film career, he is also known for his active involvement in social causes, notably his efforts in aiding the 2004 Boxing Day Tsunami and as the founder of the anti-discrimination NGO, The Foundation.
