Robby Hayes of 'The Bachelorette' Denies Making a Sex-Tape with Lindsie Chrisley

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, June 6, 2024

In a podcast with BLEAV’s “Housewives and Vanderpump,” “The Bachelorette” star, Robby Hayes, broke his silence as he confirms the accidental recording of intimacy with Lindsie Chrisley.

With attention gaining traction over the issue, 30-year old reality show star announced and clarified the existence of a sex-tape with Lindsie Chrisley. Robby Hayes explained that the said moments were caught by accident, adding that the footage was taken from a security camera.

With the on-going reaction from the public, Hayes highlighted that he has been in constant contact with Lindsie to prevent any future distribution of the said tape. Things worsen as Lindsie accuses her own father of extortion over her existing sex-tape.

Robby Hayes attends the premiere of "Tyler Perry's Boo 2! A Madea Halloween." | Source: Getty Images

Cited from a Georgia police report, Lindsie revealed that her father, Todd Chrisley, and brother, Chase Chrisley, blackmailed her of the possible release of the video if she did not agree to conceal the truth over a particular incident.

Despite such allegations, both Lindsie’s father and brother, remain keen on proving their innocence. In fact, Chase told PEOPLE that he ultimately cares for his sister, and remained confused about her current actions.

Lindsie Chrisley at the NBCUniversal Summer Press Day. | Source: Getty Images

“Not only am I heartbroken over these false allegations by my sister — and they are totally false — but so are my parents and the rest of our family,” he said.

On another note, Todd remains to portray the father figure, claiming that his actions have been for the greater good of Lindsie; pointing out his constant effort to contain any information regarding Lindsie’s extramarital affairs.

Todd Chrisley attends the 7th Annual 'Night of Generosity' Gala benefiting. | Source: Getty Images

With Lindsie’s best interest in going about her own business, it seems that she has continued to run out of luck.

Most recently, she made headlines after being accused of leaking information leading to the arrest of her father over fraud and tax evasion cases.

Musa Ghanayem, Lindsie’s spokesperson, makes it clear that Lindsie was never the source of any information. She also emphasizes that Lindsey continues to be the victim of her family’s lies, harassment, and threats.
