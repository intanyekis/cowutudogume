Who is Oh Eui-shik?

Oh Eui-Shik (오의식) is a well-known South Korean actor and television personality. Till now he has been featured in multiple drama and TV films.

He is better known for his role in the famous Korean dramas, True Beauty, Fight for my way, Hometown Cha-Cha-Cha, and Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-Joo.

Oh Eui-Shik: Professional life, Career

Oh Eui-Shik was interested in acting since he was young. To pursue his acting career, he moved to Seoul in his early twenties.

Before fame, he used to work as an office worker in the design interior field. Oh used to watch a lot of theatre world, which inspire him, and realized he too wanted to act.

Happily, in the year 2006, he made his stage debut and appeared in the children’s musical Bremen Musician. Later on, he joined the musical Rocksitter and in the year 2013, he officially became a member of the theater company, Ganda.

Moreover, Oh Eui-Shik’s debut Television series is Oh My Ghost playing the role of Choi Ji-Wung.
