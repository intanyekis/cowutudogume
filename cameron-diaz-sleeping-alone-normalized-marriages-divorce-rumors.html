Cameron Diaz Says Sleeping Alone Should Be Normalized In Marriages As She Dodges Divorce Rumor

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, June 7, 2024

Highlights

  • Cameron Diaz prefers separate sleeping arrangements in her ideal living situation with her husband, having her own room and a shared bedroom for intimate moments.
  • She stated that she made these comments before getting married and no longer feels that way due to her husband being wonderful.
  • Cameron rarely shares details about her personal life, including her daughter, but did give a rare update on her daughter's bond with her husband through music.

Cameron Diaz is infamously private about her personal life – she hasn’t even shared any images of her daughter two years after her birth. But the actress’ new comments on what she thinks is the best sleeping arrangements for partners has fans questioning if there’s trouble in her marriage.

During an appearance on the Lipstick on the Rim podcast, which aired on Tuesday, December 9, Cameron explained her ideal living situation when it comes to raising a family.

Cameron Says She Ideally Wants A House Separate From Her Husband's

“To me, I would literally, I have my house, you have yours. We have the family house in the middle. I will go and sleep in my room. You go sleep in your room. I’m fine,” Cameron revealed. “And we have the bedroom in the middle that we can convene in for our relations.”

Cameron didn’t confirm if this is similar to the current living arrangement she has with her husband Benji Madden of Good Charlotte fame. The pair have been married since 2015 and welcomed a daughter, Raddix, in 2020.

But after receiving pushback from the podcast hosts, who suggested Cameron may receive backlash for sharing her unpopular opinion, the actress said she’s made similar comments before and feels secure enough in her marriage to do so.

“I’ve already said it,” the Charlie’s Angel alum said in her defense. “By the way, I don’t feel that way now because my husband is so wonderful. I said that before I got married.”

Cameron Gave A Rare Update On Her Daughter Earlier This year

This wasn’t the only time this year Cameron gave an uncharacteristic update on her personal life. In October, she shared some details about her 3-year-old daughter Raddix to the delight of fans. While appearing on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon, Cameron revealed the sweet way Benji has bonded with their toddler.

“He has, like, the best songs,” she said, explaining Benji and Raddix share a love for music. “They play in my head all the time because he sings them to her and she knows the words, and it’s the sweetest thing ever. It really is. It kills me.”

“He has some bangers,” Cameron continued. “I’m telling you. I’m like, ‘Whoa, that is like, that’s catchy.’”

Cameron has been sharing a bit more about her family since un-retiring from acting last year, but fans shouldn’t expect to get too many detail about her personal life, especially her daughter.
