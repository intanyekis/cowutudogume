Wears Tiny Two-Piece On Neighbors 2 set Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Wearing blue bikini bottoms and a skimpy, red triangle top, Chloe was photographed partying with a gaggle of girls on what looks like the sorority house lawn. For the movie, she’ll play a college student who moves in with her sorority sisters to the house next door to Zac Efron’s fraternity — and completely wreaks havoc on the guys.

This, of course, is a similar theme to the first movie, in which Zac and his pals, including Dave Franco, tormented new parents Seth Rogen and Rose Byrne, who lived next door. This time, the college guys will have to get help from their former nemeses in order to take on Chloe and her squad. Oh, how the tables have turned!

From what we’ve seen so far, we’re going to see a lot of skin in this film! Earlier this week, both of the movie’s stars were photographed on-set wearing super short shorts to film outdoor scenes, and when Selena Gomez was seen filming over the summer, her tight pink dress left very little to the imagination.

But, if it’s anything like the first movie, we can also expect a ton of laughs. “I can’t say much, it’s under wraps,” Chloe told us EXCLUSIVELY at the Teen Choice Awards. “I can say that…be on the watch because I am crazy. I am going to do some crazy stuff in that movie. It’s gonna be really fun. Seth and all the boys, we’re gonna…It’s gonna be a little crazy.”

HollywoodLifers, are you excited to see Chloe in Neighbors 2?
