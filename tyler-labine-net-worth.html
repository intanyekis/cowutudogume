Tyler Labine Wiki Biography

Tyler Sean Labine /ləˈbiːn/ (born April 29, 1978) is a Canadian actor. He established a cult following for starring in the television series Breaker High, Invasion, and Reaper, as well as the movie Tucker & Dale vs Evil, he was a voice actor in the 2013 animation film Monsters University as Brock Pearson who is a massive bird-like purple monster with antelope-like horns and an orange beak, he is the vice-president of the Greek Council. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
