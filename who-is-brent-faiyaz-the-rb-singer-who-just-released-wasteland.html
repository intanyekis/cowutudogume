Why 'Wasteland' Singer Brent Faiyaz Is An Artist To Watch Out For

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 10, 2024

Brent Faiyaz, one of the hottest voices of the current generation of R&B artists just dropped his sophomore album Wasteland. A follow up to his 2020 EP F*ck The World, the 19-track project is executive produced by Jonathan “Freeze” Wells and features guest appearances from DJ Dahi and Tyler, the Creator on “Gravity”, and Alicia Keys on “Ghetto Gatsby”. The album also features Drake and The Neptunes on “Wasting Time”, Joony on “FYTB”, and Tre’ Amani on “Addictions”.Faiyaz released Wasteland on July 8. The singer is in the running to score his first number one album on the Billboard 200 chart. The album is expected to sell between 105,000 - 115,000 units in its first week, according to HitsDailyDouble. The chart predictors believe Wasteland will contend with Bad Bunny’s Un Verano Sin Ti for the top spot. If successful this will make Brent the first independent artist since 2018 to achieve a number one album. It would also make him the first R&B act to debut atop the chart since Still Over It last November by Summer Walker, who announced she was quitting music back in 2020. With his impending success, you are bound to hear the name Brent Faiyaz again. So who is Brent Faiyaz?

8 Who Is Brent Faiyaz?

The 26-year-old singer, songwriter, and record producer Faiyaz was born Christopher Brent Wood. He grew in popularity after he was featured on GoldLink's single "Crew" alongside Shy Glizzy in 2016, which was certified 5× Platinum by the RIAA, and earned him a nomination for the Grammy Award for Best Rap/Sung Collaboration. In his music career, he references Lauryn Hill as one of his biggest influences.

7 The Maryland Native Now Calls L.A. Home

Faiyaz moved from Columbia, Maryland to Charlotte, North Carolina in his teenage years. He subsequently relocated to Los Angeles to take his career to the next level. He also lived in Baltimore, Maryland. The multi talented artist credits his growth to him learning about himself in the different cities that he’s resided in.

6 He Named His Band And Debut Album After A Tattoo

A group called Sonder was formed in October 2016 by Faiyaz and fellow record producers Dpat and Atu, the name came from a tattoo he has over his right eyebrow. The group released their debut single, "Too Fast," the following year. At some point, he decided that he wanted to prove what he could accomplish on his own. Faiyaz’ 2017 debut album was also called “Sonder Son”.

5 What Is Brent Faiyaz’s Net Worth?

Brent Faiyaz has an estimated net worth somewhere between $600 thousand and $1.7 million as of 2022. His main source of income is from his music career; he acquired his wealth from being a successful music producer, songwriter and performer. Faiyaz likely has additional revenue sources including brand partnerships.

4 Brent Faiyaz's Billboard Impact

In 2021, Brent Faiyaz earned his first entries on the Billboard Hot 100 for collaborations with Drake and controversial lyricist Tyler, the Creator. His biggest hit “Trust” did not chart on the Billboard Hot 100, but peaked at number 13 on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 chart. The song has been certified platinum by the RIAA. “Dead Man Walking” also peaked at number six on the Bubbling Under chart. Otherwise, his feature on Juice World’s “Demonz” peaked at number 17 on the Billboard Bubbling Under Hot 100 chart and number eight on the Billboard Bubbling Under R&B/Hip-Hop chart.

3 Is Brent Faiyaz Dating?

He was rumored to have dated famous model and social media star Zahara Davis in the past. The two were supposedly together from 2018 to 2020; Davis is said to have dated Danish model Tobias Sorensen in 2018 as well. Brent Faiyaz remains relatively discreet when it comes to his love life. As of writing, Faiyaz appears to be single.

2 What Happened To His Sonder Bandmates?

Though Brent Faiyaz continues to make strides as a solo artist, Sonder hasn’t disbanded. His Debut came in 2017 but the trio released the smoky single “ One Night Only” in early 2018 and the following year they released “What You Heard”. Just last year the group released a new song "Nobody But You," featuring vocals by English singer Jorja Smith. Otherwise, Atu and Dpat were producers on Snoh Aalegra’s album Temporary Highs In The Violet Skies, which earned them a Grammy nomination for Best R&B Album at the 64th Annual Grammy Awards.

1 What Fans Are Saying About “Wasteland”

Fans are absolutely loving “Wasteland”. “I’ve heard enough. Brent Faiyaz has the album of the year with WASTELAND,” one Twitter user wrote. Another fan can’t seem to get enough writing “wasteland ain’t enough i need brent faiyaz to moan in my ears”. Wasteland currently sits at number one on the iTunes R&B chart and has already topped the chart for all genres. It is also the number one album on Apple Music’s top albums chart.
