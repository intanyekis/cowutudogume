Emma Chamberlain Wiki, Age, Boyfriend, Height, Weight, Biography, Affairs &amp; More

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Emma Chamberlain (born May 22, 2001) is an American YouTube Content Creator, Instagram Influencer, and Social Media Sensation from San Bruno, California. She is one of the most followed internet celebrities.

She has millions of followers on various social media platforms such as YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. Emma has over 8.81 million subscribers on her self-entitled YouTube channel.

Furthermore, the content creator posted her first-ever video “City Inspired Summer Lookbook 2017” on June 2, 2017. As per the source, she uploaded contents almost daily during the summer of 2017.

Internet Career, Various YouTube Videos & Net worth

Her first video became a hit and it has 1.6+ million videos as of April 2020. On July 27, 2017, Chamberlain’s video “We All Owe The Dollar Store An Apology” was the first one that went viral on the internet and garnered 4.2 million views.

Later, Chamberlain did her first collaboration with Cody Ko (Canadian YouTuber, podcaster, rapper, and comedian) on his podcast channel Insanely Chill. She also worked with Ellie Thumann, Hannah Meloche, Rhett & Link, Smosh, David Dobrik, etc.

In her successful career, the YouTuber worked with big brands such as Dote, Louis Vuitton, Snapchat, Teen Vogue, and much more.

Some of her various popular YouTube videos are Trying to Style Ugly Clothes, Learning TikTok dances at 3 am, Epic Thrift Haul, Going Insane in Hawaii, 24 hours without a phone, and much more.

She even designed a clothing line called “Low Key / High Key by Emma” with Dote in July 2018. In June 2018, Emma moved to Los Angeles, California in order to work on YouTube on a higher level.

In addition, the content also created a YouTube channel called “The Sister Squad” with fellow YouTubers James Charles and The Dolan Twins. They also got nominated for a 2019 YouTube Ensemble Shorty Award.

Earlier, Chamberlain even won the 2018 Streamy Award in the category “Breakout Creator”. The internet celebrity also launched her own weekly podcast named “Stupid Genius” in April 2019.

Emma Chamberlain’s Net worth is $2.6 million US Dollars, as of 2020 which she accumulated via Google Adsense, sponsorships, paid content, endorsements, etc.

Childhood, Parents, Siblings & Education

The talented internet star’s age is 18 years old, as in 2019. Many fans want to know Emma Chamberlain’s birthday which falls on the 22nd of May. She is very close to her father “Michael Chamberlain” and mother “Sophia Chamberlain” who raised her in San Mateo County, California, USA.

Emma has no siblings as she is the only child of her parents. when she turned 5 years old, Sophia and Michael parted their ways. As of education, the content creator studied Central Middle School.

Later, Chamberlain joined Notre Dame High School, Belmont. She also remained a member of the California All-Stars Pink cheer team. She did competitive cheer for almost five years. However, the social media star quit school in her first semester during her junior year.
