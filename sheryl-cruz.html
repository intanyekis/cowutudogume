Sheryl Cruz is a Filipino actor and singer who is known for her television roles like playing Divina Ferrer in the drama series Sinasamba Kita, Donya Valeria Del Castillo-Altamirano in the romantic drama series Rosalinda, Valeria Arevalo in the drama series Bakekang, Alice Sagrado in the fantasy drama series Luna Mystika, Miss Maria Minchin in the drama series Princess Sarah, Fabiola in the fantasy romantic drama series Grazilda, Bettina “Betty” Romero in the drama series Impostora, and Myrna Labrador in the fantasy drama series Agimat ng Agila. Sheryl Cruz’s film credits include I Have Three Hands, Mga Kuwento ni Lola Basyang, Batang Quiapo, Mano Po III: My Love, Payaso, No Retreat… No Surrender… Si Kumander, Rock-A-Bye Baby, and Ronquillo: Tubong Cavite, Laking Tondo.
