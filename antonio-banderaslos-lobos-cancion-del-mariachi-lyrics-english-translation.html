Antonio Banderas/los Lobos Cancion Del Mariachi Lyrics English Translation

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 16, 2024

The song "Cancion Del Mariachi" by Antonio Banderas/Los Lobos is a lively and passionate Spanish song that tells the story of a proud and honorable man who enjoys the finer things in life. The lyrics express his love for women, money, and love, as well as his adventurous spirit as he rides his horse through the mountains, guided by the stars and the moon. The song also highlights his love for music, particularly playing the guitar and singing the traditional Mexican "son" style, accompanied by a mariachi band. The man also enjoys indulging in his favorite drinks, such as aguardiente and white tequila with salt. Overall, the song is a celebration of life, love, music, and the joy of embracing one's passions.
