The Most Expensive Mercedes Vehicles Owned By Hollywood A-Listers, Ranked

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Quick Links

  • Floyd Mayweather Jr.’s Mercedes G63- $180,150
  • Sylvester Stallone’s Mercedes SL65 - $300,000
  • Rick Ross's Maybach 57 S - $430,000
  • Jay Leno’s Mercedes-Benz SLR McLaren - $500,000
  • Kanye West’s Mercedes SLR Stirling Moss - $1.06 Million
  • Travis Scott’s Maybach G650 Landaulet - $1.6 Million
  • Jay Leno’s 1955 Mercedes 300 SL Gullwing Coupe - $2 Million
  • Jay Z’s Maybach Exelero - $8 Million

Mercedes-Benz is a German luxury car company and has been one of the pioneers in the luxury car segment since its inception in 1926. Over the years, Mercedes has become one of the largest premium car manufacturers in the world and has launched several iconic cars. The brand has also acquired Maybach, which belongs to the luxury segment of vehicles. Mercedes cars have a starting price of around $30000 but can go up to millions of dollars, making it affordable to just premium buyers, including several A-listers from Hollywood.

Hollywood celebrities make millions of dollars from the industry and are known for their expensive spending and lavish lifestyles. Their spending primarily includes an incredible collection of posh cars, which is incomplete without a Mercedes. A-listers like Jay Z, Kanye West, Sylvester Stallone, etc., own some of the rarest and most expensive Mercedes cars in the world.

Floyd Mayweather Jr.’s Mercedes G63- $180,150

Floyd Mayweather Jr. is the world’s richest boxer, with almost $450 Million net worth. He is an avid car lover and is known to spend millions of dollars buying luxury cars. He boasts a multi-million dollar garage with exquisite vehicles like the Mercedes G63, McLaren 650S Spider, Bentley Flying Spur, Lamborghini Aventador, Ferrari Enzo, etc. The Mercedes G63 is priced at $180,150 and was gifted to Mayweather by his mother on the 4th of July, 2013, as per Complex. It flaunts a finesse matte black color and boasts of 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that can generate 577 Horsepower and accelerate from 0-60 MPH in 3.9 seconds.

Sylvester Stallone’s Mercedes SL65 - $300,000

Priced at a sky-high $300,000, the Mercedes SL65 graces the car collection of the prolific veteran actor Sylvester Stallone. At 76, he is still as enthusiastic about supercars as his younger counterparts. He boasts an awe-inducing car fleet, including a Bugatti Veyron, Mercedes-Benz G550, Ferrari 612 Scaglietti, etc. Stallone owns a limited edition Mercedes SL65 Black series, decked with a 661 horsepower AMG 6.0-liter V12 engine and a carbon fiber composite construction, making it the sportiest Mercedes-Benz car ever, as per Hot Cars.

Rick Ross's Maybach 57 S - $430,000

Rapper Rick Ross has a peculiar affinity for Mercedes-Maybach cars and even named his record label Maybach Music Group. The rapper has made big bucks with his ace rapping skills, accumulating a net worth of $45 Million. His car collection includes a Maybach 57 S, Maybach S650 Cabriolet, Rolls-Royce Phantom, Ferrari 488 Spider, etc. But with a price tag of $430,000 and only 3000 units ever produced, the Maybach 57 S is the most expensive and rarest car in Rick’s collection. The 57 S was his first ever Maybach purchase, way back in 2008, and features a massive turbocharged V12 cylinder petrol engine that can reach a top speed of 171 mph.

Jay Leno’s Mercedes-Benz SLR McLaren - $500,000

Jay Leno is a comedian and former late-night talk show host with a whopping net worth of $ 450 Million, as reported by Celebrity Net Worth. He is as famous for his car collection worth $50 Million as he is for his TV appearances. Jay Leno’s garage boasts some of the most exquisite cars in the world that, includes the likes of a Bugatti Veyron Grand Sport, Porsche 911 Carrera Cabriolet, Pagani Zonda F, Maybach Exelero, and the Mercedes-Benz SLR Mclaren. The SLR Mclaren is rumored to be Jay Leno’s favorite car in his collection which has a silver finish and is priced at half a million dollars. The vehicle boasts a carbon fiber body and a ruthless 5.4L V8 Engine that generates 617 Horsepower taking the car from 0-60 MPH in just under 3.4 seconds.

Kanye West’s Mercedes SLR Stirling Moss - $1.06 Million

Rapper, Singer, and Kim Kardashian’s ex-husband, Kanye West, is known for his extravagant lifestyle. With a staggering net worth of $398 Million, as per Forbes, Kanye West can afford all kinds of luxury, including a luxury car garage. His car collection includes several unique and expensive cars like the Porsche Panamera, Mercedes SLR McLaren, Lamborghini Aventador, etc. But the standout car in West’s incredible collection is the $1.06 Million Mercedes SLR Stirling Moss which, along with being the rarest car in his garage, is also one of the rarest cars in the world, with just 75 units built to date. He showed up to the 2011 Cannes Film Festival with the stunning Stirling Moss, powered by a 5.5-liter, supercharged V-8 engine, and flaunts a silver paint job.

Travis Scott’s Maybach G650 Landaulet - $1.6 Million

Yet another rapper on this list, Travis Scott, has an impressive net worth of $65 Million, which he earned from his successful rap career. Travis flaunts an incredible car collection that includes a Bugatti Chiron, Lamborghini Aventador, Lamborghini Urus, and the super rare Mercedes-Maybach G650 Landaulet. The Sicko Mode rapper treated himself with a white Maybach G650 in 2019, cashing out a whopping $1.6 Million. It is one of the rarest cars in the world, with just 99 ever made. Travis has heavily customized the Landaulet with a brown finish on the exterior and a mini fridge on the interior. An exotic G65 AMG twin-turbocharged V-12 engine powers the super rare car.

Jay Leno’s 1955 Mercedes 300 SL Gullwing Coupe - $2 Million

Featuring in the list for the second time due to his enormous car garage, which spans 17,000 square feet, Jay Leno also owns a super-rare 1955 Mercedes 300 SL Gullwing Coupe. The former talk-show host acquired the vintage Gullwing in 2015, hoping to restore it to its former glory. The Mercedes 300 SL is a two-seat sports car manufactured during the Gullwing Coupe from 1954 to 1957. The iconic sports car was powered by a three-liter overhead camshaft straight-six engine which could reach a top speed of 163 MPH. Jay Leno found a red Gullwing in a dilapidated condition left out in the Californian desert by its previous owners. His love for cars immediately led him to buy them out for an undisclosed amount. The full-restored version of this car has an estimated price of $2 Million in the vintage car market.

Jay Z’s Maybach Exelero - $8 Million

With a staggering $2 Billion net worth, Jay Z is the wealthiest rapper in the world and the richest celeb on this list. Being the richest, it was evident that he would also own the most expensive car on this list. Jay Z’s had the Maybach Exelero custom-built for him in 2013, and it was previously owned by rapper Bryan ‘Birdman’ Williams. The car is priced at a whopping $8 Million and is the most expensive celebrity car in the world. The Exelero featured a futuristic black paint job reminiscent of the Batmobile and was featured in Jay Z’s 2009 music video Lost one. The beast of a car features a twin-turbocharged V-12 engine that can easily zoom from 0 to 62 mph in just 4.4 seconds, reaching a top speed of 218 mph.

Hollywood A-Listers, with their enormous incomes and affinity for cars, own some of the most exquisite Mercedes cars in the world. The prices of the vehicles range from $180,150 to a whopping $8 Million. Rappers have dominated this list, with Jay Z, Kanye West, and Travis Scott owning some of the rarest cars. Jay-Z stands out with his Mercedes-Maybach Exelero being the most expensive car on this list.
