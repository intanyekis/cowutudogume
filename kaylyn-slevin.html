Getting To Know About Kaylyn Selvin-The Chicken Girls Actress

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Kaylyn Selvin is an American actress, Model, gymnast who has risen her fame through the famous web series Chicken Girls. She has also starred in many movies.

She is also a winner of MSA Dance Miss Malibu Teen 2018 and went on to compete in Miss Utah Teen USA 2019.

Childhood of Selvin

The actress of Chicken girls was born in the year 2000 on December 28, in the Chicago of the United States. She was raised and grew up there only.

She was fond of acting from her childhood. She also likes to dance and is interested in outdoor games too.

Parents and siblings

Selvin is the daughter of William Selvin and Kristyn Selvin. She shares a good bond with her father and mother as shown in her social media post.

She has not shared any information regarding her siblings yet with any of the media. She has started her career after she has completed her education.

Personal life of Selvin

The young and energetic Kaylyn always makes her fan amazed with her stunning and bold looks. She has always been a hardworking and dedicated personality.

She is also a Trained Gymnast and cheerful leader at a young age which is a really inspiring thing for the upcoming generations.

Height and weight

The beautiful actress MSA Dance Miss Malibu Teen 2018 winner has an alluring body with a height of 5 ft 6 inches and weighs 50kg.

She has blonde color hair with blue eyes which have added more beauty to her.

Career Highlight

The beautiful actress Selvin has given many web series and movies through her incredible acting skill.

Her memorable builds include Ass Backward, Double Income, No Kids( D.I.N.K), The Incybers, My Freakin Family, etc.

Hobbies and interest

The competitor of the famous show Miss Utah Teen USA 2019 is fond of dancing, Gymnast and yoga, listening to music, etc.

She also likes to travel and explore new things and ideas from different places in her vacation days.

The relationship status of Selvin

The bold actress Kaylyn is single at present. She has not shared any information regarding her boyfriend in any of the media yet.

The net worth of Selvin

The Trained Gymnast has a total net worth of $2 million. She has access to such an amount through her successful career as an actress, dancer, model, and Gymnast.

She does not own any personal business or other kinds of stuff that generates money.

Rumor and controversies

Though she has not officially claimed about her relationship with Mycah Pittman yet to any of the media.
