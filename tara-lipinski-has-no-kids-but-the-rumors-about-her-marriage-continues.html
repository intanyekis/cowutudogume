Tara Lipinski Has No Kids But The Rumors About Her Marriage Continues

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 17, 2024

The rumors and speculations about Tara Lipinski, the sports commentator, of having children are dispersing across several internet portals.

During Kamila Valieva’s free skate performance at the Beijing Winter Games, Tara Lipinski and Johnny Weir kept the light of their typically fun and intelligent comments.

Lipinski, Weir, and co-commentator Terry Gannon were quiet during Valieva’s uncharacteristically mistake-filled routine, other than to note the jumps the 15-year-old was attempting.

Tara and Weir were vocal in their opposition to the Court of Arbitration for Sport’s decision to allow Valieva to compete after she failed a drug test.

Fans Ask About Tara Lipinski Children As The Rumors About Her Marriage Continues

Tara Lipinski has denied the rumors about having children and cleared the misconception about the marriage between her and Johnny Weir. Netizens claimed that the speculations about Tara and Jonny started because of their chemistry on the commentary table.

Lipinski married sports producer Todd Kapostasy in June 2017 after two years of dating. Lipinski and Kapostasy first met in May 2015, when Lipinski gave Kapostasy an award at the Sports Emmys.

The Olympic gold winner and broadcaster had married Todd Kapostasy in Charleston, South Carolina.

Johnny Weir, a close friend of Tara, had served as a groomsman at the wedding. She handed her buddy a crystal-encrusted boutonniere that matched his similarly studded shoes perfectly.

Johnny is a competitive figure skater from the United States. Weir is a two-time Olympian, a World bronze medalist in 2008, a two-time Grand Prix Final bronze medalist, the 2001 World Junior Winner, and a three-time US National champion.

Tara Lipinski Family Update

Patricia and Jack Lipinski welcomed Tara Lipinski to their family on June 10, 1982, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

Jack Lipinski is an oil executive and lawyer. Lipinski’s father was promoted at work in 1991, and the family relocated to Sugar Land, Texas, near Houston.

She honed her skills in the Houston Galleria, an upmarket shopping center with a public skating rink. Lipinski and her mother traveled back to Delaware in 1993.

Her father stayed in Texas to support the family and to resume her training with coach Jeff DiGregorio, who had worked with Lipinski on and off for three years before their trip to Texas.

Lipinski and her mother moved to Bloomfield Hills, Michigan in 1995 to train at the Detroit Skating Club with instructor Richard Callaghan. Tara’s grandparents were born in Poland.

Tara Lipinski Career Details

Tara Lipinski has a decorated career as an actress, former competitive figure skater, and sports pundit from the United States.

According to IMDB, Tara has 19 on-screen acting credits. Her career was brief in the 1990s, but it was one of the best that any professional skater could hope for. Tara is a two-time Champions Series Final champion, a 1998 Olympic champion, and a 1997 U.S. national champion.

She became a sports pundit after retiring from competitive sports in 1998. She also presented skating contests.
